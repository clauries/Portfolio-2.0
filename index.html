About Me

I am a web developer in training, currently enrolled in the University of Oregon's Coding Bootcamp. With an B.S. degree in counseling, I have a passion for helping individuals, families, and teams utilize developmental tools for growth. Technology already plays a crucial role in our lives, providing opportunities right in the palms of people's hands. My dream is to partner with companies and organizations to create better tools for personal and team development.


A Portland, Oregon native, I'm a lover of stormy days where I'm curled up in a coffee or tea shop, working away on a project. Perhaps on one such day I'll have the pleasure of meeting with you to strategize how we can use tech to grow yourself and/or your teams.
